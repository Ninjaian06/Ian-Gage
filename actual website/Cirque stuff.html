Cirque stuff

I have been a fan of Cirque for almost a year and a half.
I was introduced to it's music through last years marching band show called "La Nouba" based off of the show of the same title, and after researching the marching band compositions, I was hooked.

Here is a list of my favorite cirque du soliel shows

  1. Quidam

    This is my favorite show because of the music, which is an amazming mix of jazz and hard rock. Its story is more darker and has a grounded storyline, which immerses you into the story's world.
  2. Varekai

    I'm a fan of this show because its music is unique with having the ability to tell a convincing narritive through music. I also really enjoy the the set design of a lot of characters, because they all stand out and are truely unique to this show.
  3. La Nouba

    This is the show that started my love for cirque, and it still holds up to this day, with its amazing jazz and blues styled music.
